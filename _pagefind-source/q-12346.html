You are given a sorted array X[0..n-1] representing house coordinates on a line. You may place exactly k post offices such that each office serves a consecutive block of houses. The cost for a block [l..r] is sum_{t=l}^r |X[t]-X[m]| where m is the median index of the block. Return the minimum total distance and the endpoints of the k blocks. Propose a DP with dp[i][p] = best cost to cover first i houses with p offices, including transitions, base cases, and reconstruction, and analyze time/space?
