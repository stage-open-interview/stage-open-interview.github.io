Design a dynamic interval data structure that maintains a multiset of closed intervals [L, R] with weights and unique ids. Support insertInterval(L, R, w, id), eraseInterval(id), and updateIntervalWeight(id, w). Implement queries at a point x: (a) totalWeightAt(x) = sum of weights of all intervals covering x, and (b) kthLargestWeightAt(x, k) among those intervals. Duplicates allowed. Aim for amortized O(log n) per update and O(log n) per query. Sketch data layout and correctness?
