Grid Dispatch: An n×n city grid with m drivers. Each batch of B online updates moves drivers to new cells (driver_id, new_x, new_y). After each batch, compute the sum over all cells of the Manhattan distance to the nearest driver. Design a practical data structure with amortized analysis: preprocessing, per-batch update time, memory, two optimizations, and a comparison to recomputing the distance transform from scratch after every batch. Explain asymptotics in n, m, B, and T?
