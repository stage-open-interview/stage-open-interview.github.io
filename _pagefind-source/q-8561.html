In a tiny form-validation engine, four fields F1..F4 take values from {TRUE, FALSE, UNKNOWN}. Constraints: (F1 ∧ F2) → F3, (F2 ∨ F4) → F1, and ¬F4 → (F1 ∨ F3). Is there an assignment with at least one UNKNOWN such that none of the constraints is FALSE? Outline a simple 3-valued backtracking propagation and give one concrete satisfying assignment?
