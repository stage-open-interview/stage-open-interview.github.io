Given a string s of length n consisting of lowercase letters and an integer k (0 <= k <= n). You may perform at most k edits, where one edit changes any character to any other lowercase letter. After edits, consider the longest palindromic subsequence (LPS) of the edited string. Your task is to maximize the LPS length by choosing edits, and return both the maximum LPS length and one exemplar palindromic subsequence that attains it. Provide a DP formulation with dp[i][j][t] representing the maximum LPS length in s[i..j] after at most t edits, including transitions and reconstruction?
