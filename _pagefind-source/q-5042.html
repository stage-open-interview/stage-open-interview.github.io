Maintain a directed graph G=(V,E) with nonnegative weights. Edges arrive online in batches of size B (no deletions). After each batch, design a (1+ε)-approximate SSSP oracle from a fixed source s to all nodes. State (i) preprocessing time to initialize distances from s, (ii) amortized update time per batch to update the oracle, (iii) query time dist(s,v) for any v, (iv) total memory, (v) two practical optimizations, and (vi) a comparison to re-running Dijkstra from s after every batch. Give asymptotics in n, m, B, ε?
