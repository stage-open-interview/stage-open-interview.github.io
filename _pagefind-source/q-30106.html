Given an array profits of length n, you may move from index i to i+1, i+2, or i+3 as long as the destination exists. Each visited index contributes profits[i] to your total. Compute the maximum total you can accumulate when reaching index n−1 and the number of distinct paths achieving this maximum, modulo 1e9+7?
