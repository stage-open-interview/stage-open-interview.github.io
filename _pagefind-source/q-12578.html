Design a data structure to maintain a dynamic set of weighted intervals on a line. Each interval [L, R] has a weight w and a unique id. Supported operations: addInterval(id, L, R, w), removeInterval(id), updateIntervalWeight(id, newW). Queries: sumWeightsAtPoint(x) giving the total weight of all intervals covering x, and kthLargestWeightAtPoint(x, k) returning the k-th largest weight among intervals covering x. Aim for amortized O(log N) updates and O(log^2 N) queries. Explain data layout and update steps?
