You're given a string s of length n (lowercase). You may select exactly k non-overlapping contiguous substrings that are palindromes. For each block [l..r], you gain (r-l+1). Return the maximum total length and the endpoints of the k blocks. Propose a DP with isPal[l][r] precomputation and dp[i][t] = max total length from i with t blocks; transitions: skip i or pick a palindrome [i..j] with isPal[i][j], value = (j-i+1) + dp[j+1][t-1]. Include reconstruction and complexity?
