Design a beginner-friendly concurrency exercise: an IoT telemetry collector for 200 devices. Each device's readings must be processed in device-order; readings from different devices may be processed in parallel. Implement per-device FIFO buffers feeding a bounded global queue and a small worker pool. When backlog grows above a threshold, coalesce by keeping only the last 5 readings per device and dropping the rest. Test with bursts, verify per-device ordering, backpressure, degradation, and graceful shutdown?
