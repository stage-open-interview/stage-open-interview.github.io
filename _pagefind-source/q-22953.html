Design a RollingTopKData structure that ingests events of the form (bucket_id, key_id, value, ts). For a sliding window W seconds, maintain per-key sums and per-bucket totals. Implement: 1) addEvent(ts, bucket_id, key_id, value) in O(log N), 2) topKKeys(K, currentTime) returning the K keys with largest sums within window in O(K log N), 3) topKBuckets(K, currentTime) returning the K buckets with largest totals in window in O(K log B). Use arrays, linked lists, a hash table, and a heap; handle expirations lazily; discuss space/time tradeoffs?
