You're given a string s of digits, length n, and an integer k. Split s into exactly k non-empty, non-overlapping substrings that cover s, each substring must be a palindrome. Each substring is interpreted as a decimal integer (no leading zeros unless the substring is exactly '0'). Maximize the sum of the k numbers and return the boundaries of the substrings in addition to the maximum sum. Provide a DP recurrence and reconstruction plan?
