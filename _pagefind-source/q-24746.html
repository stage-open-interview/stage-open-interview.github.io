You maintain a dynamic collection of closed intervals on [0, T). Updates arrive in batches of B; each update is either add [l, r) or delete a previously added interval [l, r). After each batch, decide whether the union of all intervals equals [0, T] (i.e., full coverage). Propose a data structure that (i) preprocesses in O(T), (ii) handles each batch in O(B log N) amortized, (iii) answers in O(1) whether full coverage holds, (iv) uses near-linear space, (v) include two optimizations, (vi) compare to recomputing coverage after every batch. Provide asymptotics in T, B, N?
