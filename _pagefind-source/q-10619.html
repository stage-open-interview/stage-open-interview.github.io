You're given an n x m grid of non-negative integers. Start at (0,0), move only right or down to (n-1,m-1). You may apply a boost to at most k cells along the path, doubling that cell's value in the sum. Propose a DP with dp[i][j][t] as the max sum to (i,j) using t boosts, transitions from top/left with/without boosting the current cell, base cases, and reconstruction. Time: O(n*m*k); Space: O(n*m*k)?
