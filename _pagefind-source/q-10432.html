In 2D, you have a dynamic set of points with insertions only, arriving in batches of size B. After each batch, compute the number of points on the upper convex hull. Propose a practical data structure and algorithm that (i) inserts B points and updates the hull in amortized O(B log n) time, (ii) answers hull size in O(1), (iii) uses near-linear space, (iv) includes two optimizations, and (v) compares to rebuilding the hull after every batch. Provide asymptotics in terms of n=|P|, B?
