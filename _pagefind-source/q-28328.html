In a multi-tenant CNF gateway on Kubernetes, design a per-tenant dynamic traffic shaping feature controlled by a central control plane. For each tenant, a policy exists with fields: enabled (bool), latencyMs (int), and percentTraffic (0-100). The sidecar retrieves the policy via a lightweight REST call from a cluster-local control plane, caches it with a TTL, and applies per-request latency jitter within [0, latencyMs] for a percentage of traffic. Include rollout, fail-safe on control-plane downtime, and provide a minimal namespace-scoped test harness?
