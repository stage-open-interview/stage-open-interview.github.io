Design a data structure to manage a dynamic multiset of circular intervals on a fixed circular timeline with period P. Each interval is represented as [start, end) on the circle; intervals may wrap around and duplicates are allowed. Implement insertInterval(start, end, w), eraseInterval(start, end, w) (one occurrence), rangeSumInArc(a, b) returning the total weight of all intervals intersecting arc [a, b] clockwise, and kthLargestInArc(a, b, k) returning the k-th largest weight among intervals intersecting that arc. Target average O(log n) per operation. Explain handling wrap-around, coordinate compression, memory trade-offs, and duplicates?
