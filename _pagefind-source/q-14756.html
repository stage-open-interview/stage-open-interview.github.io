You maintain a categorical distribution of n items across m categories. You receive online batches of B updates where each update reassigns one item i from its current category to a new category. After each batch, output the entropy H = - sum_j (f_j/n) log(f_j/n). Propose a data structure and amortized analysis to support (i) O(1) preprocessing, (ii) O(B) updates per batch, (iii) O(1) entropy query, (iv) near-linear space, (v) two optimizations, and (vi) a comparison to recomputing H from scratch after every batch. Give asymptotics in n, m, B?
