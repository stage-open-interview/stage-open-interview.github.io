Maintain a dynamic set P of 2D points with online insertions in batches of size B. After each batch, determine whether any two points lie within distance D of each other. Propose a practical data structure and an amortized analysis that (i) inserts efficiently, (ii) answers the yes/no query in O(1) or near-constant time, (iii) uses near-linear space, and (iv) includes two optimizations and a comparison to rebuilding from scratch after every batch. Express asymptotics in n=|P|, B, D, and coordinate bounds?
