Design a dynamic interval-weighted set on a 1D line. Intervals [l, r] carry weight w and a unique id; support insertInterval(l, r, w, id), eraseInterval(id), and updateWeight(id, newW). Provide efficient queries: sumWeightsAtPoint(x) returning the total weight of all intervals covering x, and kthLargestWeightInRange(l, r, k) returning the k-th largest weight among all intervals that intersect the range [l, r]. Scalable to up to 2e5 intervals with coordinate compression?
