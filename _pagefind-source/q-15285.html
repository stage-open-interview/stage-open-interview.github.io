In an n×m grid of non-negative rewards A[1..n][1..m], compute the maximum total reward by choosing exactly k non-overlapping monotone paths from (1,1) to (n,m). Each path yields sum of visited cell values, minus a fixed cost c per path. Paths must be pairwise disjoint (no cell visited by more than one path). Return the maximum total reward and the k paths' routes. Provide a DP formulation and reconstruction plan?
