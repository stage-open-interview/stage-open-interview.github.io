In a 3-valued rule engine with predicates P1..P5 ∈ {T,F,U}, add a Maj operator defined as Maj(X,Y,Z) = T if at least two inputs are T, F if at least two inputs are F, else U. Constraints: 1) Maj(P1,P2,P3) = P4, 2) (P4 ∨ P5) → P1, 3) (¬P2) ∨ (P5 ∧ P3). Is there an assignment with at least one U such that all constraints evaluate to T or U? Provide one such assignment and outline a 3-valued backtracking search?
