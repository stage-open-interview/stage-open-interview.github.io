After each batch, update the union of all intervals and report the total covered length. Propose a practical data structure that supports batch inserts, with preprocessing, amortized per interval insertion time, memory bounds, two optimizations, and a comparison to recomputing the union from scratch after every batch. Provide asymptotics in N, B, and coordinate range?
