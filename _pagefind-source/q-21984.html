You have a text T of length n. Process updates in batches of B substitutions (set T[i] = c). After each batch, report the number of occurrences of a fixed pattern P of length m in T. Design a practical incremental data structure that (i) preprocesses in O(n), (ii) updates in amortized O(B log n) time, (iii) answers counts in O(1), (iv) uses near-linear space, (v) includes two optimizations, and (vi) compares to rebuilding with a full scan after every batch. Provide asymptotics in terms of n, m, B?
