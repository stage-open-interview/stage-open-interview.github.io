In a tiny access-control policy, four conditions Ingest, Normalize, Train, Serve take values in {T, F, U}. Constraints: (Ingest ∨ Normalize) → Train, (Train ∨ Serve) → Ingest, and (¬Serve) → (Normalize ∨ Train). Is there an assignment with at least one U such that none of the constraints evaluates to F? Propose a simple 3-valued backtracking propagator and illustrate with a concrete satisfying assignment?
