Maintain the convex hull of a 2D point set where points are added only in batches of B (no deletions). After each batch, report the number of hull vertices and the hull area. Propose a practical incremental hull maintenance structure that (i) preloads the existing hull, (ii) updates amortized per batch, (iii) provides O(1) queries for count and area, (iv) uses near-linear space, (v) includes two optimizations, and (vi) compares to rebuilding from scratch after every batch. Provide asymptotics in terms of n, B, h?
