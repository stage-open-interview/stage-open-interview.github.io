You are given an n x m grid profits[i][j] of non-negative integers. From (0,0) to (n-1,m-1) you can move only right or down. You may apply up to p power-ups on at most p cells along the path, each power-up doubling that cell’s value. Return the maximum possible profit and the path. Propose a DP with dp[i][j][t] representing max profit to (i,j) using t power-ups, plus recurrences, base cases, and reconstruction?
