Edges are only inserted online in batches of size B; no deletions. After each batch, specify: (i) preprocessing time and space to build distances from every landmark, (ii) amortized update time per batch to update the oracle, (iii) query time for dist(u,v), (iv) total memory, (v) two practical optimizations, (vi) a comparison to rebuilding all-pairs distances after each batch. Provide concrete asymptotics in terms of n=|V|, m=|E|, k, ε, B?
