Given an array nums of length N and an integer K, compute the maximum sum obtainable by choosing exactly K non-overlapping subarrays (contiguous) of nums. Each chosen subarray contributes its sum to the total; subarrays must be disjoint and ordered left-to-right. Provide both a top-down memoization approach and a bottom-up tabulation approach with O(NK) time and O(NK) space, and discuss edge cases (K > possible, negative values)?
