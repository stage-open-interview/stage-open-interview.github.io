You are given an array arr[0..n-1] of integers representing daily revenue. You may schedule exactly k ad campaigns on non-overlapping blocks of consecutive days, with at least one day gap between campaigns. Each campaign [l..r] yields revenue equal to sum(arr[l..r]). Return the maximum total revenue and the endpoints of the k campaigns. Propose a DP formulation with dp[i][t] representing the best revenue using first i days with t campaigns, include recurrences, base cases, and reconstruction, and discuss time/space?
