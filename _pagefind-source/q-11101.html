In a directed graph G=(V,E) with n up to 2e5, edges arrive in online batches of size B (no deletions). After each batch you must answer reachability queries for arbitrary pairs (u,v) in O(1). Design an incremental transitive-closure structure that (i) preprocesses in O(n^2/word_size) using bitsets, (ii) updates amortized per batch to O((n/word_size)·B), (iii) stores reachability as a bitset per vertex, (iv) uses near-linear space, (v) include two optimizations, (vi) compare to recomputing closure after every batch. Provide asymptotics in terms of n, m, B?
