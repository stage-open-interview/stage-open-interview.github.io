You are given an n x m grid of nonnegative integers rewards[i][j]. You start at (0,0) and may move only right or down to reach (n-1,m-1). You may make at most T turns (direction changes). What is the maximum sum of rewards along the path? Provide a DP formulation with state dp[i][j][t][dir], recurrences, base cases, and a reconstruction plan?
