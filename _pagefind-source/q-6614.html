After each batch, output the total number of ordered pairs (u,v) such that v is reachable from u, counting (u,u). Propose a practical incremental transitive-closure scheme with: (i) preprocessing, (ii) amortized per-batch update time, (iii) O(1) query for the total reachable pairs, (iv) memory bounds, (v) two optimizations, (vi) a comparison to rebuilding reachability from scratch after every batch. Provide asymptotics in n and B?
