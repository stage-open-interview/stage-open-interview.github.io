Design a persistent data structure for a dynamic multiset of 2D points with weights. Support: insertPoint(x,y,w,t) and erasePoint(x,y,w,t) (duplicates allowed). Provide two queries as of time t: (a) sumWeightsInRectangle(x1,x2,y1,y2,t) returning total weight of points in the rectangle active at time t, and (b) kthLargestWeightInRectangle(x1,x2,y1,y2,t,k) returning the k-th largest weight among active points in the rectangle at time t. Target amortized O(log^2 n) updates and queries; discuss coordinate compression, persistence/versioning, and data layout (e.g., persistent 2D segment tree or 2D Fenwick with per-node weight structures)?
