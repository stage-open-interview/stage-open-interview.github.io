You're given an array daily[0..n-1] of non-negative integers representing daily revenue. You may run at most k non-overlapping advertising campaigns; each campaign must cover a contiguous window of days. The net value of a campaign on window [l..r] is sum(daily[l..r]) - C, where C is a fixed cost per campaign. Return the maximum total revenue and the endpoints of the chosen windows. Provide a DP formulation with dp[i][t] = max revenue using first i days with at most t campaigns, transitions for ending a campaign at i, base cases, reconstruction, and time/space analysis?
