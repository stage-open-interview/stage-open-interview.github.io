In a battery-management system, five status bits B1..B5 take values in {T, F, U}. Constraints: (B1 ∧ B2) → B3, (B2 ∨ B4) → B5, and (¬B5) → (B1 ∨ B3). Is there an assignment with at least one U such that none of the constraints evaluates to F? Outline a simple 3-valued backtracking propagator and provide a concrete satisfying assignment if exists?
