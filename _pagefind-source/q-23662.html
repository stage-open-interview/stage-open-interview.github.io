Streaming motif counting: edges arrive in batches of B in a growing graph G with n nodes; after each batch, count exactly the occurrences of a fixed small pattern P (e.g., a 4-node motif) as a subgraph. Propose a streaming algorithm that (i) preprocesses once, (ii) updates per batch in O(B·poly(p)) time, (iii) reports the total count in O(1) per batch, (iv) uses near-linear space, (v) includes two optimizations, and (vi) compares to recomputing from scratch after every batch. Provide asymptotics in n,m,p,B?
