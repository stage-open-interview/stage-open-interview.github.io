You're maintaining a multiset of N labeled items drawn from U distinct labels. Updates arrive in batches of size B; each update is insert x or delete x (delete x guaranteed to exist). After every batch, determine whether any label occurs at least F times. Propose a data structure that (i) builds in O(N), (ii) processes each batch in amortized O(B), (iii) answers in O(1) whether max frequency >= F, (iv) uses near-linear space, (v) two optimizations, and (vi) compare to rebuilding histogram after every batch. Provide asymptotics in N, B, F, U?
