Given an N x M grid of integers, start at (0,0) and reach (N-1,M-1) in exactly k moves. From (i,j) you may move to (i+1,j), (i,j+1), or (i+1,j+1). The score is the sum of visited cells (including start and end). Implement a memoized DFS and a bottom-up tabulation to return the maximum sum, and discuss time/space trade-offs and memory-optimized variants?
