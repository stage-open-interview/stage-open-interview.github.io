In a tiny access-control policy graph, four attributes A,B,C,D take values in {T,F,U}. Constraints: (A → B) ∨ (C ∧ ¬D), (B ∨ D) → A, and (¬C) → (A ∨ D). Is there an assignment with at least one U such that all constraints evaluate to T or U (not F)? Provide a 3-valued backtracking plan and a concrete satisfying assignment?
