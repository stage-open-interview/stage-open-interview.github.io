Scenario: In a weighted undirected graph G=(V,E) with nonnegative weights, edges arrive in online batches of size B. From a fixed root r, maintain a (1+ε)-approximate shortest path tree (SPT) to all nodes after each batch. Propose a practical scheme that (i) initializes the SPT, (ii) updates after a batch with amortized time, (iii) bounds memory, (iv) offers two optimizations, and (v) compares to rebuilding the SPT from scratch after every batch. Provide asymptotics in n=|V|, m=|E|, B, ε?
