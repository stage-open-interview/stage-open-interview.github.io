Given an array A[1..n] of integers (positive/negative) and an integer k, select exactly k non-empty, non-overlapping contiguous segments such that at least one index separates any two segments. The score of a segment [l..r] is the sum of A[l..r]. Return the maximum total score and the endpoints of the k segments. Provide a DP formulation with dp[i][t] as the best total for first i elements using t segments, include transitions, base cases, and reconstruction?
