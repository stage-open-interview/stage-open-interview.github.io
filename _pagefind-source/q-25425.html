You have a stream of keys arriving in batches of size B, appended to an unbounded log. You must maintain the exact top-k most frequent keys over all seen items. After each batch, propose a data structure and the amortized update costs that achieve O(B log k) per batch and O(k) retrieval, with near-linear memory. Compare to recounting from scratch after every batch. Express asymptotics in terms of n total items, k, and B?
