You're given a string s of length n consisting of lowercase letters. Choose exactly k non-overlapping substrings that maximize the total length, with the constraint that each chosen substring can be rearranged to form a palindrome (i.e., its character counts allow at most one odd count). Return the maximum total length and the endpoints of the chosen substrings. Propose a DP formulation with dp[i][t] = max total length for first i chars using t substrings, including a fast feasibility test for s[p..i], base cases, and reconstruction plan; discuss time/space?
