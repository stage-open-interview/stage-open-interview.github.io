You maintain N items with non‑negative weights w[i]. Updates arrive in batches of size B; each update is (i, delta) meaning w[i] += delta or w[i] -= delta. After each batch, determine whether two distinct indices i != j exist with w[i] + w[j] >= T. Propose a data structure that (i) preprocesses in O(N), (ii) handles each batch in amortized O(B log N), (iii) answers in O(1) whether such a pair exists, (iv) uses near-linear space, (v) provide two optimizations, and (vi) compare to recomputing from scratch after every batch. Provide asymptotics in N, B, T?
