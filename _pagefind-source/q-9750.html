Is there an assignment with at least one U such that none of the constraints evaluates to F under Kleene 3-valued semantics (¬ maps T<->F, U→U; ∧, ∨ as min/max; → defined as ¬X ∨ Y)? Show a concrete depth-3 backtracking trace that yields a satisfying assignment, or prove none exist?
