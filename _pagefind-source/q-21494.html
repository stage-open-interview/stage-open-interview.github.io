Dynamic directed graph with n nodes. Edges insert in online batches of size B (no deletions). After each batch, decide if there exists a path from S to T with length at most L. Propose a practical incremental index to solve this: (i) one-time near-linear preprocess, (ii) amortized per-batch updates, (iii) O(1) query time, (iv) near-linear space, (v) two optimizations, and (vi) a fair comparison to rebuilding shortest paths after each batch. Provide asymptotics in n, m, B, L?
