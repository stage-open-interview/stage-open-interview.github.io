You're given an array A of length n and an integer k. Find a non-contiguous subsequence of length exactly k that is non-decreasing (A[i1] <= A[i2] <= ... <= A[ik]), maximizing the sum of its elements. Return the maximum sum and the indices of the chosen elements. Propose a DP: dp[i][t] = max sum of a non-decreasing subsequence of length t ending at i. Recurrence: dp[i][1] = A[i]; dp[i][t] = A[i] + max_{j<i, A[j] <= A[i]} dp[j][t-1]. Include reconstruction, base cases, and time/space complexity?
