In a weighted directed graph modeling a data-processing pipeline, edges arrive only in batches of size B (no deletions). After each batch, design a practical index to answer, for any v in V, whether dist(s, v) ≤ D (D fixed) in O(1) time, with: (i) preprocessing, (ii) amortized per-batch update, (iii) near-linear space, (iv) two optimizations, (v) a comparison to rebuilding distances each batch. Provide asymptotics in n=|V|, m=|E|, B, D?
