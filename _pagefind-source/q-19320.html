Maintain a streaming 2D Pareto frontier: you start empty and receive online batches of B points (x,y) with x,y ≥ 0. After each batch, output the current Pareto frontier F (maximizing both coordinates): no point outside F dominates a point in F. Propose a data structure and algorithm that (i) supports batched insertions efficiently, (ii) yields amortized update cost per batch, (iii) lists the frontier in O(|F|) time, (iv) uses near-linear space, (v) includes two optimizations, and (vi) compares to rebuilding the frontier after every batch. Provide asymptotics in n and B?
