In a two-world Kripke structure {w1, w2} with atoms {P, Q, R}, valuations at each world are in {T, F, U} under Kleene three-valued semantics. Accessibility: w1→{w1,w2}, w2→{w2}. Constraints: 1) □(P ∧ ¬Q) ∨ R is T at w1, 2) ◇P → □Q is F at w1, 3) If R is U at w2 then P is not F at w2. Is there a valuation for P, Q, R at w1 and w2 that satisfies all constraints? Outline a 3-valued modal backtracking strategy and give a concrete satisfying assignment if any?
