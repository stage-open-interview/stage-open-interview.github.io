You maintain a weighted undirected graph G=(V,E) with nonnegative weights. Edges arrive online in batches of size B. You need to answer s shortest-path queries dist(u_i,v_i) for i=1..s after each batch, within a (1+ε) approximation, reusing work across all pairs. Propose a single shared data structure (i) initialization to support all s pairs, (ii) amortized per-batch update cost when a batch arrives, (iii) per-query time, (iv) memory, (v) two practical optimizations, and (vi) a comparison to rebuilding from scratch per batch. Express asymptotics in n=|V|, m=|E|, s, B, ε?
