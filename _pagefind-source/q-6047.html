Given a directed graph G=(V,E) with n=|V|, edges inserted online in batches of size B (no deletions), after each batch determine if G contains a directed cycle. Propose a practical incremental cycle-detection structure: (i) preprocessing time, (ii) amortized update time per batch, (iii) cycle-detection query time, (iv) memory usage, (v) two optimizations, and (vi) a comparison to re-running a topological sort after every batch. Provide asymptotics in terms of n, m, B?
