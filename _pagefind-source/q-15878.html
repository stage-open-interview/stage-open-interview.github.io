You have a directed graph with n nodes; edges can be added or removed in online batches of size B. After each batch, compute the exact number of nodes reachable from a fixed influencer t within at most H hops. Propose a practical incremental structure that (i) precomputes a compact reachability index, (ii) updates amortized per batch for both insertions and deletions, (iii) answers the count in O(1) or near, (iv) uses near-linear space, (v) provides two optimizations, and (vi) compares to rebuilding reachability from scratch after every batch. Give asymptotics in n, m, B, H?
