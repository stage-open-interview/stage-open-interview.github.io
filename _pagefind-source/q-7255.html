Each window yields revenue sum(A[l_j..r_j]) * (r_j - l_j + 1). Return the maximum total revenue and the endpoints of the k windows. Propose a DP formulation with dp[i][t] representing the max revenue for first i days using t windows, the recurrence, base cases, and a reconstruction plan?
