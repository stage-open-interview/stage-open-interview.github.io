Pod B has a node-affinity rule preferring Node Type=compute-optimized. When you schedule both pods, Pod A ends up on a node with only 2 cores available (due to other pods), causing constant CPU throttling. How would you diagnose and resolve this scheduling conflict while ensuring both pods are co-located and performing optimally?
