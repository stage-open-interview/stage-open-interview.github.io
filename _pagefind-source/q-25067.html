Maintain N numbers in a multiset. In each batch of B updates, you either insert x or delete x (guaranteed to exist). After each batch, determine whether the median of the multiset is at least T. Propose a practical data structure that (i) preprocesses in O(N log N) or better, (ii) handles each batch in amortized O(B log N), (iii) answers in O(1) whether median >= T, (iv) uses near-linear space, (v) two optimizations, and (vi) compares to re-sorting after every batch. Provide asymptotics in N, B?
