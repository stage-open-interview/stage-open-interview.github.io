Design a pluggable macro-expansion engine for a DSL used in a data-pipeline. Macros like map, filter, or aggregate can be registered at runtime by name; they transform an AST node or a sub-tree and produce a new AST. The engine must support hygiene (shadowed identifiers, isolation between macro contexts), composability of macros, and robust error reporting with source positions. Provide minimal interfaces, a registry, and a usage example in TypeScript?
