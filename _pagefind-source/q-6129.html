You maintain an undirected graph with n nodes and m edges; edges are inserted in batches of size B (no deletions). Each edge has a weight w. After each batch, output the total weight of the current minimum spanning forest (MSF). Propose a practical incremental MSF data structure with: (i) fast preprocessing, (ii) amortized per-batch updates, (iii) O(1) MSF weight query, (iv) near-linear space, (v) two optimizations, (vi) a comparison to rebuilding MSF from scratch after every batch. Provide asymptotics in n, m, B?
