You’re given an N x M grid with non-negative values. Start at (0,0) and move to (N-1,M-1) by only right or down. You have energy E; each step costs 1 energy. Some cells are power-ups that reset energy to E, usable at most R times in the path. Maximize the sum of visited cell values. Propose a DP with states dp[i][j][r][e], recurrences, base cases, and reconstruction; discuss time/space and optimizations?
