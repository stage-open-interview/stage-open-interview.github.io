Problem: In an n x m grid with some blocked cells (grid[i][j] = 1 means blocked). From (0,0) to (n-1,m-1) you may move only right or down. You cannot land on blocked cells, and you cannot take more than L consecutive moves in the same direction. Return the number of valid monotone paths modulo 1e9+7. Implement both a memoized DFS and a bottom-up DP; discuss edge cases (start or end blocked, L large, small grids) and runtime?
