Maintain a fixed-size window: a stream of integers, window size W. In each batch of B new elements, append them and advance the window by B positions (dropping the oldest B elements). After every batch, report the number of distinct values currently in the window. Propose a data structure with: (i) preprocessing in O(W), (ii) amortized updates in O(B), (iii) O(1) query for the distinct count, (iv) near-linear space, (v) two optimizations, and (vi) a comparison to recomputing from scratch after every batch. Provide asymptotics in W and B?
