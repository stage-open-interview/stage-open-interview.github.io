Given an n x m grid grid[i][j] of integers, you start at (0,0) and move to (n-1,m-1) by only right or down moves. You must visit exactly k turns (a turn is when your move direction changes from right to down or from down to right). Return the maximum possible sum of visited cells and one path achieving it. Provide a DP formulation with dp[i][j][t][dir] representing the max sum to (i,j) with t turns and ending in dir (0 for right, 1 for down); include transitions, base cases, and reconstruction details?
