You manage a booking system with N reservations, each as an interval [L_i, R_i] on a 1D time axis. In online batches of size B, you receive updates that replace the endpoints of B intervals (i -> [L'_i, R'_i]). After applying the batch, report the maximum number of concurrent reservations at any time and a coordinate where this maximum occurs. Propose a practical data structure and amortized analysis, including preprocessing time, memory, and a comparison to recomputing from scratch after every batch. Provide asymptotics in N, M (distinct endpoints), B?
