You’re given an n x m grid of integers. Start at (0,0) and move only right or down to (n-1,m-1). You have K shields. Each shield lets you step on a negative-valued cell without reducing your total (the cell's value counts as 0 for that step). You cannot exceed K shields. Return the maximum sum along a path and one optimal path’s moves. Provide a DP formulation with states dp[i][j][k], base cases, recurrences, and reconstruction?
