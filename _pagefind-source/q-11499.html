In a policy automation scenario, four signals S1..S4 take values in {T, F, U}. Define XOR(X,Y) := (X AND NOT Y) OR (NOT X AND Y) under Kleene semantics. Constraints: 1) (S1 XOR S2) -> S3 2) (S2 XOR S4) -> (NOT S1) 3) (S3 XOR S4) -> (S1 OR S2) Is there an assignment with at least one U such that none of the constraints evaluates to F? Provide a depth-3 backtracking trace and one concrete satisfying assignment?
