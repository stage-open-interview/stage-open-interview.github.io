Three propositions A,B,C take values in {T,F,U}. Define a 3-valued XOR3: if either input is U, XOR3(a,b)=U; else XOR3(a,b)=T iff a≠b, otherwise F. Constraints: (A XOR3 B) → C, (B XOR3 C) → A, (C XOR3 A) → B. Is there an assignment with at least one U such that none of the implications evaluates to F? Outline a simple 3-valued backtracking propagation and give a concrete satisfying assignment?
